Sector 01 Solar Power Plant Boss: SectorGuard

▸
Main ObjectiveRestore base power → Reboot main power terminal → Reach the top floor → Defeat SectorGuard
Key AreasGenerator Entrance (Block 01) → Power Distribution Center (Block 02) → The Concourse (Block 03)
CollectiblesSafe Box ×3, Pure Lunum ×1, Mod ×1, REM ×2, Cartridge Holder ×1, Storage Expander ×1, Mini Cabin Figure ×3
Estimated Time~1.5–2.5 hours (100% collectibles)

Walkthrough Highlights

  1. Enter the Generator Entrance and follow the path to learn basic controls: movement, hacking tutorial, basic shooting
  2. Reach the Power Distribution Center and face the 5-lock gate. Jump to the left side of the gate and take the elevator down for Safe Box #1
  3. At the 5-lock gate corridor entrance, look up and hack the elevated terminal to disable all laser barriers. Jump down into the former barrier area, clear enemies, and collect Safe Box #2
  4. Unlock all 5 electronic locks and enter The Concourse
  5. Trigger the SectorGuard boss fight in the Concourse (see Boss Guide for detailed strategy)
Backtracking Tip: Safe Box #3 and the Pure Lunum require the Lim Eraser obtained from Sector 2 to collect. In the Power Distribution Center, take the elevator up → jump over the left railing → use Lim Eraser to clear the blue Lunafilament seal door → defeat multiple enemy waves and collect.

Sector 02 Mass Production Array Boss: Creator

▸
Main ObjectiveRide the tram → Hack 5 beacons → Unlock the giant gate → Defeat Creator
Key AreasTest Site Entrance → Shopping District → Connecting Passage → Commercial District → Reclamation Control Center → Comm Tower Plaza
CollectiblesSafe Box ×3, Pure Lunum ×2, Mod ×2, REM ×3, Cartridge Holder ×1, Mini Cabin Figure ×3
New AbilityLim Eraser (unlocked in Block 05) — clears blue Lunafilament obstacles

Walkthrough Highlights

  1. Ride the tram to the "Fake NYC" area and hack the first beacon in the Times Square zone
  2. Navigate through the Shopping District and Commercial District, hacking all 5 beacons. Use rooftops and alleys to avoid patrolling enemies
  3. Obtain the Lim Eraser in Block 05 Reclamation Control Center — a core backtracking tool
  4. Once all beacons are hacked, the giant gate unlocks — enter the Comm Tower Plaza and trigger the Creator boss fight
Recommendation: After obtaining the Lim Eraser, immediately return to Sector 1 to collect Safe Box #3 which was previously inaccessible. Make sure you have enough Repair Canisters before the boss fight.

Sector 03 Terra Dome Boss: Garden Keeper

▸
Main ObjectiveRide the tram → Find Eight → Restart 2 Lim Regulators → Head underground to restart the Lim Regulator → Defeat Garden Keeper
Key AreasTerra Dome Entrance → Eco Modeling Lab → Central Hall → Soil Research Wing → Terrarium
CollectiblesSafe Box ×3, Pure Lunum ×2, Mod ×3, REM ×4, Training Data ×2, Mini Cabin Figure ×3

Walkthrough Highlights

  1. Arrive at the Eco Modeling Lab and restart the first Lim Regulator. Note: Plant-type enemies are weak to fire-element weapons
  2. Head to the Plant Factory to restart the second Lim Regulator
  3. After the story encounter with Eight, proceed to the underground Terrarium area
  4. Restart the underground Lim Regulator and trigger the Garden Keeper (giant robot) boss fight

Sector 04 Lunum Mines Boss: Air Raider → LunaDigger

▸
Main ObjectiveRide the tram → Restart IDUS → Head to Nexus Tower → Infiltrate the warehouse → Defeat Air Raider → Cross the ruins → Storm Nexus Tower → Defeat LunaDigger
Key AreasMine Entrance → Crane Operation Yard → Warehouse → Main Excavation Site → Nexus Tower
CollectiblesSafe Box ×3, Mod ×3, REM ×3, Pure Lunum ×2, Training Data ×2, Storage Expander ×2, Mini Cabin Figure ×3

Walkthrough Highlights

  1. The mine area is wide open — make full use of cranes and mining equipment as mobile platforms to traverse the expansive space
  2. Infiltrate the warehouse to trigger the Air Raider fight — use mining drills for mobility, standard hack-damage cycle
  3. Cross the ruins area and prepare for the sector's final boss, LunaDigger
⚠ Warning: LunaDigger is the mid-game difficulty wall. Phase 1 has an extremely short hack window (Auto Hack is virtually required). In Phase 2, you can hack small drones to counterattack the boss. See the Boss Guide for details.

Sector 05 Experimental Pragmatics Linear · No Collectibles

▸
Main ObjectiveHead to the experimental area → Rescue Diana → Obtain the energy reactor → Escort Diana back to the Shelter
New AbilityCleanse — Hold LB/E to charge and release, dispelling red Lunafilament obstacles
ImportantThis sector cannot be returned to via fast travel.

Walkthrough Highlights

  1. This is a purely linear story segment with no collectibles — focus on narrative progression and emotional impact
  2. Diana temporarily leaves the party and Hugh must fight alone. Enemy density is high — manage ammunition carefully
  3. After obtaining Cleanse, you can clear red Lunafilament, which is the key to unlocking hidden rooms across many previous areas
  4. During the Diana escort phase, prioritize eliminating ranged enemies
⚠ Before Leaving: This sector has no missable content, but after obtaining Cleanse, immediately backtrack to previous areas to clear red Lunafilament and collect any missed items.

Sector 06 Central Port Boss: Sentinel → Eight/Idea → Abiosis

▸
Main ObjectiveRide the tram → Meet up with Eight → Reach the core area → Head to the orbital elevator → Purify the Lunafilament source → Hack 2 terminals → Triple boss gauntlet
Key AreasPort Entrance → Research Wing → Regolith Lab → Lunafilament Lab → Orbital Elevator
CollectiblesSafe Box ×3, Mod ×3, REM ×3, Training Data ×2, Pure Lunum ×2, Storage Expander ×1, Mini Cabin Figure ×3

Walkthrough Highlights

  1. This is the final sector — ensure all weapons are fully upgraded and stock up on Repair Canisters
  2. In the Lunafilament Lab, use Cleanse to purify the source, then hack the terminals on both sides
  3. The final gauntlet includes: Sentinel → Eight/Idea → Abiosis — no save points in between
  4. Defeat Abiosis to watch the default ending
True Ending Hint: To unlock the hidden true ending, you must first obtain the Black Box Mod in "Unknown Signal" mode, equip it, and then defeat the final boss again. See the Tips & Endgame page for details.

Epilogue Final Showdown

▸

Use the Eraser to defeat Abiosis's final form and follow Diana to complete the game. The Epilogue is purely cinematic with no collectibles.

Post-game unlocks: Unknown Signal mode, New Game+, Chapter Select (for backtracking collectibles).
