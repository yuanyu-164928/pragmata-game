Essential Beginner Tips

The 8 Most Important Survival Rules for Your First 3 Hours

1

Hack First, Shoot Later — The Golden Rule

Unhacked enemies are nearly immune to bullets. Build muscle memory — whenever you encounter any enemy, have Diana hack it first, then open fire.

2

Return to the Shelter Often

The Shelter is the only place where you can fully restore HP, replenish Repair Canisters, upgrade firmware, print new weapons, and spend Cabin Coins. Head back after completing each Sector.

3

Prioritize Unlocking Object Scan

Print the Object Scan module at the Shelter's Unit Printer as early as possible — it highlights collectibles, upgrade components, and hidden paths, dramatically reducing missed items.

4

Upgrade Firmware Evenly

Level Hugh's exoskeleton, primary weapon unit, and Diana's hacking capabilities evenly to around level 10 before specializing. Over-specializing too early can cause roadblocks in certain stages.

5

Backtrack Immediately After Gaining New Abilities

The Lim Eraser (Sector 2) and Cleanse (Sector 5) can clear obstacles in previous areas and unlock hidden rooms. Go back and explore as soon as you get them.

6

Save Overdrive for Critical Moments

A full Overdrive Protocol bar simultaneously opens all nearby enemy weak points and freezes them. Save it for boss stun windows or phase transitions — don't waste it on regular enemies.

7

Spend All Materials Before Boss Fights

Before every boss fight, use up all your upgrade materials at the firmware terminal. Prioritize your main weapon — don't spread your investments too thin.

8

Best Early-Game Combo

Stasis Net to immobilize → Thrusters to close distance → Shockwave Gun point-blank. Extremely effective against most elite enemies and early-game bosses.

Hidden Ending & Post-Game Content

True Ending Unlock Route + Unknown Signal Mode Explained

🔓

True Ending Unlock Steps

  1. Complete the main story and watch the default ending
  2. Enter "Unknown Signal" mode
  3. Achieve 100% collectible completion in all 5 main Sectors
  4. Defeat all 5 enhanced MK-II Bosses
  5. Complete all 10 simulation challenges in the Hidden Chamber
  6. Obtain the Black Box Mod
  7. Equip the Black Box Mod and defeat the final boss Abiosis again
  8. Watch the True Ending
📡

Unknown Signal Mode

Unlocked after completing the game, this is your gateway to the true ending:

  • Freely select any Sector for backtracking and collectible hunting
  • Each Sector's boss is upgraded to MK-II versions with more attack patterns and higher damage
  • The Hidden Chamber contains 10 simulation challenge stages — essentially combat arenas
  • It is recommended to play Unknown Signal mode before starting New Game+
🔄

New Game+

Carries over all weapons, firmware levels, and collectible progress. Enemy difficulty is increased, but you can steamroll early stages with endgame gear. Perfect for filling in missed items and experimenting with different builds.

Best Cabin Coin spending order via Bingo mode: Mods → Cosmetics → Robot Data → Training Simulator.

⚠ Note: Completing a specific puzzle in Sector 3 without taking damage unlocks a hidden optional boss: Rogue Sentinel. It is recommended to challenge this boss only after fully maxing out your firmware.
