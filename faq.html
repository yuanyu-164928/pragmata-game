Frequently Asked Questions

The most common questions from new players

What kind of game is PRAGMATA?
A sci-fi third-person action-adventure game developed by Capcom using the RE Engine. The core is the dual-character system — Diana handles hacking to expose weaknesses, while Hugh handles weapon-based damage output. Main story takes 10–15 hours to complete; 100% completion takes approximately 20 hours.
What platforms is it on? Does it support English?
PC (Steam), PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and Switch 2. Full multi-language support including English voice acting and subtitles is available at launch.
Can I go back for missed collectibles?
Yes. After completing the game, Chapter Select is unlocked, allowing you to freely revisit any Sector. You can also return to previous areas mid-campaign after obtaining the Lim Eraser and Cleanse abilities.
Can a pure hacking or pure shooting build clear the game?
A pure shooting build will be extremely difficult because unhacked enemies have very high damage reduction. At minimum, go for a hybrid build — the game is designed to require using both.
Is there multiplayer or co-op?
PRAGMATA is a purely single-player narrative experience. There is no multiplayer or co-op mode of any kind.
How many endings are there? How do I unlock the true ending?
2 endings: the default ending and the hidden true ending. To unlock the true ending, you must obtain the Black Box Mod in "Unknown Signal" mode, equip it, and defeat the final boss again. See the Tips & Endgame page for details.
