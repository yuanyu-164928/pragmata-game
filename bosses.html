Weakness Analysis, Phase Mechanics & Recommended Loadouts

🔴 SectorGuard — Solar Power Plant Sentinel

Sector 1 · The Concourse (Top Floor)
Phases2 Phases (switches at 50% HP)
WeaknessOrange fuel tank on its back (must be exposed via hacking first)
Recommended LoadoutStasis Net, Shockwave Gun, Decode nodes
Core StrategyDodge the charge attack → Diana hacks → circle behind and shoot the fuel tank → reposition. Phase 2 adds missile barrages (red circles on the ground) — keep moving laterally and tap dodge when exiting the circles. The boss crouches and leans forward before charging — side-dodge immediately when you see this tell.
Burst WindowPhase 2: Stasis Net immobilize → Decode nodes → Overdrive Protocol → unload on the fuel tank

🟡 Creator (Giant Robot) — Mass Production Array Ruler

Sector 2 · Comm Tower Plaza (must hack 5 beacons to unlock the gate first)
WeaknessFace; lower weak point exposed after a successful hack
Recommended LoadoutCharge Piercer, Shockwave Gun, Sticky Bombs, Overdrive Protocol
Core StrategyMaintain mid-range distance. Destroy hacking jammers on the limbs and head first to unlock Diana's hack lock-on. After a successful hack, the lower weak point is exposed — focus fire. The boss's arm sweeps have wide reach — dodge toward the attack to use invincibility frames. Keep moving laterally during laser attacks.
Ultimate Attack CounterWhen the boss charges up, the entire arena turns red — climb the Lunafilament pillars that spawn in the arena to dodge it; failing to dodge reduces your HP to 1. After defeating it, you must complete a timed hacking sequence — failure means instant death.

🟢 Garden Keeper (Giant Plant Robot) — Terra Dome Guardian

Sector 3 · Underground Terrarium
WeaknessHead, lower body, tail (identify the currently exposed part based on body and tail movements)
Recommended LoadoutFire-element weapons (bonus damage vs. plant-type enemies), Charge Piercer, Homing Missiles
Core StrategyHack first, then attack. The boss has large but clearly telegraphed attacks. Attack the head when it lowers its head; attack the tail after a tail sweep. The lower weak point is briefly exposed when the boss is stunned.

⚪ Air Raider — Mine Warehouse Boss

Sector 4 · Warehouse
Core StrategyUse mining drills scattered around the arena as mobile platforms and cover. Use Homing Missiles to lock on while the boss is airborne. Once it lands, follow the standard hack → damage cycle. Watch out for fall hazards at the edges of the arena.
Recommended LoadoutHoming Missiles, Stasis Net, Charge Piercer

🟣 LunaDigger — Mid-Game Difficulty Wall

Sector 4 · Nexus Tower Underground Den
Phases2 Phases (switches at 50% HP)
Phase 1 WeaknessMouth only (briefly exposed when the boss surfaces or charges)
Phase 2 WeaknessMouth + entire body
EssentialAuto Hack — the manual hacking window is extremely short, virtually required
Recommended LoadoutCharge Piercer, Photon Laser, Homing Missiles, Sticky Bombs
Phase 1 StrategyThe boss spends most of its time burrowed underground, only exposing its mouth when surfacing or charging. The moment you see the mouth — hack → OPEN → high-damage weapon output. After several successful hacks, the boss is stunned — close in for a Critical Shot.
Phase 2 StrategyThe entire body is vulnerable, but the boss adds missiles and small burrowing drones. Key tactic: hack the drones to make them attack the boss — approximately 2000 damage per drone. Dump Homing Missiles on the boss when it stands still.
RewardShelter License Key 4 — unlocks advanced Unit Printer upgrades and Cabin Club Director Board

🟠 Eight / Idea — Final Stage, Second Battle

Sector 6 · Orbital Elevator Upper Level
WeaknessPurple head (exposed when glowing yellow-orange after hacking)
Core StrategyCleanse to purify → aim for the head → unload firepower. Idea will attempt to bind Hugh — perform a counter-hack when the red indicator line turns red. In the final phase, watch for red target circles on the ground and keep moving.

Epilogue · Lunar Surface
PhaseSingle phase, arcade-style shooting set piece
